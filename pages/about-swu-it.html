About the College of IT

Southwestern University PHINMA's College of Information Technology is dedicated to producing competent, innovative, and industry-ready IT professionals.

Our Mission

To provide quality information technology education that develops competent professionals equipped with technical skills, critical thinking, and ethical values. We are committed to fostering innovation through hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading college of information technology in the Visayas, recognized for producing globally competitive IT professionals who drive technological innovation and contribute meaningfully to society.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision, program, and interaction within the College of Information Technology.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in teaching, learning, and professional practice across all our programs.

Innovation

We embrace emerging technologies and creative thinking to prepare students for a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Integrity

We uphold ethical conduct, transparency, and responsibility in the use and development of technology.

Collaboration

We foster teamwork, peer learning, and strong ties with industry partners to create well-rounded professionals.

Service

We are committed to contributing our knowledge and skills to the betterment of our communities and society.

Academic Programs

Programs Offered

Choose a program tailored to your goals — from a comprehensive bachelor's degree to a career-ready associate program.

4-Year Program

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

A comprehensive program that develops highly skilled IT professionals with deep knowledge in software development, networking, database management, and emerging technologies.

Key Focus Areas
Web Development Mobile Development Network & Security Data Science Software Engineering Cloud Computing
Career Paths
  • Software Developer / Engineer
  • Web & Mobile App Developer
  • Network / Systems Administrator
  • Data Analyst / AI Engineer
  • IT Project Manager
2-Year Program

Associate in Computer Technology

A two-year technical program designed for students who want to enter the IT workforce quickly with solid foundational skills in computer hardware, networking, and business applications.

Key Focus Areas
Computer Hardware Networking Fundamentals Office Applications Technical Support Programming Basics
Career Paths
  • IT Support Specialist
  • Computer Technician
  • Help Desk Technician
  • Office / Administrative IT Staff
  • Junior Network Technician

    Our Advantages

    Why Choose SWU-IT?

    Discover what makes the SWU-PHINMA College of Information Technology the right choice for your future in tech.

    Modern IT Laboratories

    Our fully equipped computer labs with high-performance hardware and licensed software provide a professional learning environment.

    Industry-Experienced Faculty

    Our instructors bring real-world IT experience into the classroom, bridging the gap between academic learning and industry practice.

    Strong Industry Partnerships

    We maintain active partnerships with top tech companies for internships, mentorships, and job placement opportunities.

    Hands-on Project Learning

    Capstone projects, hackathons, and real-world case studies ensure students graduate with a strong portfolio of practical work.

    Certification Programs

    We prepare students for globally recognized certifications including Cisco, Microsoft, and other industry-standard credentials.

    Career Placement Support

    Our dedicated career services team connects graduates with top employers through job fairs, networking events, and placement assistance.

    Credentials

    Accreditation & Recognition

    Our programs are officially recognized and accredited, ensuring your degree meets national and international standards.

    CHED Recognized

    All IT programs are recognized and approved by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) of the Philippines.

    PACUCOA Accredited

    Our programs hold PACUCOA accreditation, a testament to our commitment to continuous quality improvement in education.

    Cisco Networking Academy

    As an authorized Cisco Networking Academy, we offer students access to world-class networking courses and certifications.

    Microsoft Partner

    Students benefit from Microsoft's academic programs, tools, and resources through our institutional partnership.
