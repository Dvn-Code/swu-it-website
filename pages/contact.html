Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and admissions.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admissions portal or visit our admissions office. The application process includes submitting requirements, taking the entrance exam, and an interview. For more details, visit our Admissions section.

What are the tuition fees for IT programs?

Tuition fees vary per program and academic year. For the most accurate and updated information, please contact our admissions office or visit the Admissions section for a detailed breakdown of fees and payment options.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, SWU PHINMA offers various scholarships including academic excellence awards, athletic scholarships, and financial assistance programs. Contact the Scholarship Office for more information on eligibility and requirements.

What career opportunities await IT graduates?

Our IT graduates have a 95% employment rate and work as software developers, network security specialists, data analysts, IT consultants, and more. Many are employed by top tech companies both locally and internationally.
